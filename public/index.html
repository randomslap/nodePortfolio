About Me

Hello, my name is Kent Okazaki and I am a web developer. I am currently taking a Full-Stack web development bootcamp course at the University of California San Diego by TrilogyEd. I have a background in programming languages such as Java and C++. Due to my curiosity, I introduced myself into programming through creating community addons for video games and "rooting" android devices during my four years of highschool. I voluntarily created addons for games such as ArmA2 and ArmA3. In my sophomore year of community college, I took a Java class for a semester. Through my journey of growing up, I have always been into techonology. I decided to look into web development because I was curious to see what it takes to create a great website. Moreover, I wanted to challenge myself and learn more about the aspect of programming.
